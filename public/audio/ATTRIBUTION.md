# Audio Attribution

All audio in this directory is CC0 (public domain). Provenance is retained here
per Growve's asset policy even though the licence does not require credit.

## Ambient — `forest-ambience.ogg` / `.mp3`
- Original title: **Forest Ambience**
- Source page: https://opengameart.org/content/forest-ambience
- Licence: CC0 1.0
- Processing: Trimmed to 30s seamless loop, gentle loudness normalisation
  (loudnorm I=-24 LUFS), 1.5s crossfades at loop boundaries, re-encoded to
  Vorbis Q3 (ogg) with an MP3 96 kbps fallback for iOS.

## Ambient — `birds-wind.ogg` / `.mp3`
- Original title: **Birds and Wind — Ambient** (from “Birds and Wind Ambient (Birds, Wind, and Synth)”)
- Source page: https://opengameart.org/content/birds-and-wind-ambient-birds-wind-and-synth
- Licence: CC0 1.0
- Processing: Ambient-only stem selected, gentle loudness normalisation
  (loudnorm I=-24 LUFS), re-encoded to Vorbis Q3 (ogg) with 96 kbps MP3 fallback.

## Footsteps — `step-leaves-1.ogg`, `step-leaves-2.ogg`, `step-gravel.ogg`
- Original title: **Different Steps on Wood, Stone, Leaves, Gravel and Mud**
- Source page: https://opengameart.org/content/different-steps-on-wood-stone-leaves-gravel-and-mud
- Licence: CC0 1.0
- Processing: Individual step samples extracted from the source archive
  (`leaves01`, `leaves02`, `gravel`), no other processing. Pitch variation
  is applied at runtime.

## Leaf rustles — `rustle01.ogg` … `rustle20.ogg`
- Original title: **20 Rustles (Dry Leaves)**
- Source page: https://opengameart.org/content/20-rustles-dry-leaves
- Licence: CC0 1.0
- Processing: FLAC source converted to mono Vorbis Q1 for mobile bundle size.
  Volume and stereo pan are randomised at runtime.

## Tend bell — `bell.ogg` / `.mp3`
- Original title: **Pleasing Bell Sound Effect**
- Source page: https://opengameart.org/content/pleasing-bell-sound-effect
- Licence: CC0 1.0
- Processing: Attenuated by −4 dB so the tend cue reads as a soft chime
  rather than a game reward. Re-encoded to Vorbis Q2 with MP3 fallback.
